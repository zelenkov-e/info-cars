Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 28, 2025

info4cars respects and protects the privacy of all users of our mobile application.

Information Collection

The info4cars app uses the device's camera solely to scan Vehicle Identification Numbers (VINs) and automatically paste the scanned code into an input field for user convenience.

The app also uses user authentication to allow users to securely access features.

  • info4cars does not collect, store, or upload any images, VIN codes, or scanned data.
  • Authentication is used only to verify access; we do not store or use personal information beyond authentication purposes.
  • All camera scanning happens locally on your device.

Authentication

We require users to log in to ensure secure and personalized access to the app. Depending on your authentication method (e.g., email, phone), basic login credentials may be handled by a trusted third-party authentication provider.

info4cars does not access or store users' passwords or personal authentication details.

Data Sharing

info4cars does not share any personal data, scanned information, or authentication data with third parties, except as necessary for authentication services.

User Control

Since info4cars does not store user-generated data or scanned VIN codes, users retain full control over their information locally on their device.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

info4cars may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on our website.
